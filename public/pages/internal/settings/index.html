About DayDreamX

DayDreamX is a Project that aims to bring a new browsing experience to users by providing a modern and customizable interface that is both fast and secure.
Our browser is heavily inspired by Opera GX and Arc, and we aim to provide a similar experience to our users without the downsides of spyware or lack of support.
We aim to provide a similar browser remake similar to r58playz's AboutBrowser, Cohen's Velocity, and Other Projects.
We keep our site running with ads, while giving you the choice to disable them.
